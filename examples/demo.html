Terminology Cards

core term

threshold

/ˈθreʃ.həʊld/
阈值；触发动作电位所需的临界水平
When membrane potential reaches threshold, voltage-gated sodium channels open rapidly and initiate an action potential.
当膜电位达到阈值时，电压门控钠通道会迅速打开并启动动作电位。
method

artifact

/ˈɑː.tɪ.fækt/
伪迹；由测量系统或运动产生的非生理信号
In electrophysiology, artifacts must be separated from biological signals before interpreting neural activity.
在电生理学中，必须先把伪迹与生物信号区分开，才能解释神经活动。
exam phrase

excitability

/ɪkˌsaɪ.təˈbɪl.ə.ti/
兴奋性；细胞或网络对刺激产生反应的能力
Changes in excitability can alter whether the same stimulation intensity produces a measurable response.
兴奋性的变化会影响同样强度的刺激是否能够产生可测量的反应。

Exam-Ready Short Answers

English answer

An action potential is a rapid, all-or-none change in membrane potential caused by coordinated opening and closing of voltage-gated ion channels.

动作电位是膜电位快速的、全或无式的变化，由电压门控离子通道协调开启和关闭所导致。
English answer

Signal preprocessing is necessary because raw recordings often contain noise, artifacts, and baseline drift that can distort interpretation.

信号预处理是必要的，因为原始记录通常包含噪声、伪迹和基线漂移，这些都会扭曲解释。
